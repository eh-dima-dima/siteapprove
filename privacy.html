Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

1. Introduction

Dilettante-lab ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal data of our users. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the Dilettante-lab platform, and applies to all users who connect their TikTok accounts through our service.

Our services operate in full compliance with TikTok's Developer Terms of Service and TikTok's Data Use and Privacy Policy. We encourage users to review those policies in addition to this document.

2. Data Collection

Dilettante-lab collects user data exclusively through TikTok's official OAuth 2.0 API. We do not use web scraping, automated bots, or any unauthorized methods to access TikTok data.

When you connect your TikTok account, we request access to the following scopes:

API Scope Data Accessed Purpose
user.info.basic Display name, avatar, username Account creation and identification within our dashboard
user.info.profile Bio, profile link Profile verification — ensuring users manage the correct account
user.info.stats Follower count, likes, video views Analytics dashboard and performance tracking
video.publish Publish action Posting scheduled videos to TikTok at the user-specified time
video.upload Upload action Uploading videos to TikTok Drafts for native editing

We do not collect data beyond what is required by the scopes listed above. We do not access your TikTok messages, contacts, or content from other users.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data collected through TikTok's API strictly for the purposes of providing the services you have requested, including:

Scheduling and publishing video content to your TikTok account at the times you specify. Uploading video content to your TikTok Drafts. Displaying your account information and performance metrics in your personal dashboard. Identifying and verifying your connected TikTok account.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the direct functionality of Dilettante-lab.

4. Data Sharing

Dilettante-lab does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties. Your data is used strictly for service provision and is never disclosed to advertisers, data brokers, or any external entity.

We may share data only in the following limited circumstances: (a) when required by law or valid legal process; (b) with service infrastructure providers (such as cloud hosting) who are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it solely on our behalf.

5. Data Storage & Retention

Video files that you upload through Dilettante-lab are stored temporarily on our servers only for the duration necessary to complete the scheduled post. Videos are automatically deleted from our servers after successful publication to TikTok, or upon your explicit request — whichever comes first.

Account metadata (such as your username and scheduling preferences) is retained for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all associated data is permanently removed within 30 days.

6. Data Deletion & Revoking Access

You have the right to delete your data and revoke Dilettante-lab's access to your TikTok account at any time. You can do so using either of the following methods:

Method 1 — Revoke via TikTok: Open TikTok → Settings and Privacy → Security and permissions → Manage app permissions → Remove "Dilettante-lab".

Method 2 — Request Account Deletion: Send an email to dmytro.yachichko@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". We will process your request and permanently delete all associated data within 15 business days.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL), secure server infrastructure, and restricted access controls. While no system can guarantee absolute security, we are committed to maintaining the integrity and confidentiality of your information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by posting the updated policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of Dilettante-lab after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions, concerns, or data-related requests, please contact us at: dmytro.yachichko@gmail.com
